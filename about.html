What is Lobster Token?

Lobster Token (LOBSTER) is a community-driven memecoin built on the Tempo Network. Our mission is to create a fun, engaging, and transparent cryptocurrency experience that brings people together through the power of community and blockchain technology.

To create the most engaging and community-focused memecoin ecosystem on Tempo Network.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the mint price?

The mint price is fixed at 0.01 USDC.e for 10,000 LOBSTER tokens.

Is there a limit to how many times I can mint?

No, there are no limits. You can mint as many times as you want, as long as you have enough USDC.e balance.

When will trading be available?

Trading functionality is currently under development and will be available soon. Follow our social media for updates.

Is the contract verified?

Yes, the smart contract is fully verified on the Tempo blockchain explorer.
